Project Overview

Advanced Sports Rehab, a respected physical therapy practice, was operating with a website from 2004 that had no SSL certificate, no mobile optimization, and an outdated aesthetic that didn't reflect the quality of care they provide.

We met Dr. Neal Mesnick at our local gym and recognized that his practice needed a digital presence that matched the excellence of his rehabilitation services.

Our challenge was to completely modernize their web presence while strengthening their brand messaging to attract new patients and better serve existing ones.
